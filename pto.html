PTO

How does PTO look here?
We have a weird PTO system.
We give our employees new laptops so they can work from anywhere no matter whenever and wherever.
If you're sick you can contact your boss and they will set up your sick time system.
We believe that if you can't come but still want to get paid if your PTO days are up you can! Although if you're very sick you will get payed.
Our system works like this; You have 30 PTO days and if you dont come you automatically use them. If you're sick but not extremely you dont use them unless you dont work.
